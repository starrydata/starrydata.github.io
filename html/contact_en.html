Collaboration & Student Admission

For inquiries regarding collaborative research, please contact us via email with an overview of your research content and objectives.
Laboratory visits are accepted at any time. Please contact us in advance by email.
Yukari Katsura also serves as an Associate Professor at the International Materials Innovation Degree Program, Graduate School of Pure and Applied Sciences, University of Tsukuba, and accepts students pursuing master's and doctoral degrees through the University of Tsukuba-NIMS Joint Graduate School Program.
For application guidelines, please refer to the University of Tsukuba-NIMS Joint Graduate School.
Prior to application, please contact us to discuss your research topic.

Access

By Train

  • Get off at Tsukuba Station on the Tsukuba Express, then take a bus for about 10 minutes
  • Get off at Arakawaoki Station on the JR Joban Line, then take a bus for about 30 minutes

By Car

  • About 15 minutes from Sakura-Tsuchiura IC on the Joban Expressway
  • About 10 minutes from Tsukuba-Chuo IC on the Ken-O Expressway
